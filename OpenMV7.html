OpenMV7 Slimelight

  • Using EDA pcb designer to exchange FT232RL board with the SP3223EEA-L/TR transceiver
  • Designing housing case and mount with custom strain relief for robot vision system
  • Soldering the components onto the PCBs

Instead of using the default Limelight product that FRC teams used, my team made a custom slimelight for the robot's visions systems. I updated the schematic to replace the FT232RL board with a SP3223EEA-L tranciever. This slimelight was used on the competition robot in 2022.
