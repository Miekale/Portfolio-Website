Toyota Design Challenge 2023

❮ ❯
  • Developed an object detection model using pretrained YOLO V8 to distinguish between three similar object classes.
  • Utilized a dataset labeled with Roboflow for the 2023 Toyota design challenge, involving detecting stickers on holes under various conditions.

The 2023 Toyota design challenge required competitors to detect whether a hole was covered by a sticker, partially covered, or there was no sticker on the hole. To make the problem challenging holes and stickers similar sizes, mandated that the camera must detect the stickers from different angles and that oneâ€™s model must be able to detect the stickers on different colored surfaces.

Addressing the challenge, we created a labeled dataset of 400+ images, implementing transfer learning with YOLO v8. Semi-supervised learning techniques were applied by using model predictions for training. The model achieved 82% accuracy on stickers, 89% on holes, and 92% on partially covered holes. It demonstrated recognition across dynamic camera positions and on red, white, and metallic surfaces.
